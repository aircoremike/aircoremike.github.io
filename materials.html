Materials

Aircore Bearings has developed a proprietary manufacturing process that enables the bonding of materials once considered unweldable. This approach delivers greater stability and a more uniform, balanced structure than traditional welding methods. No other supplier offers hollow balls in such a broad range of materials with the same level of quality. Browse our material library below.

Copper

Exceptional electrical and thermal conductivity, excellent formability, and inherent antimicrobial properties.

Tungsten

Outstanding density and strength, extreme heat resistance, and excellent wear and creep performance.
