Take Off Without the Trade-Off.

Solid, but heavy. Most large bearing balls offer strength you’ll never use — at a cost you shouldn’t have to pay. Ready to rethink the standard?

Designed specifically for weight-sensitive applications, Aircore’s hollow bearing balls combine strength, precision, and reduced mass.

Like our 440C stainless steel variant — engineered for the unique operational demands of air freighters.

Our hollow balls are available in a broad selection of metal and ceramic materials, with customizable sizes, grades, and wall thicknesses to match your application’s exact load and weight specifications.

Applications

Aerospace - Ball Transfer Units (Omnidirectional Bearings)
Defense - UAVs and UGVs, Shaped Charges, Penetrators
Automotive - CV Joints, Ball Plungers, Ball Detent Clutches
Oil & Gas - Rotary Seals and Couplings, PDC & Roller Cone Bits, Ball Check Valves

Aircore Advantages

  • Load capacity and bearing weight can be customized by adjusting the wall thickness
  • Wide variety of available materials including exotic metals and ceramics
  • Homogenous microstructures
  • Tight weight tolerances
