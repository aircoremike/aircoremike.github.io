The world's finest lightweight bearings balls


Large bearing balls are heavy, but they don’t need to be. Many applications that require large bearing balls don’t require the tremendous load-bearing capacity they offer, so most of the material of a solid steel bearing ball is wasted.
You could opt for a lighter material, like titanium or aluminum, but these materials are either too expensive or aren’t hard enough. Or you could choose a hollow ball, but material options and load capacity were limited, until now.


Aircore Bearings offers the world’s first and only hollow 440C stainless steel bearing balls. Our proprietary manufacturing process allows customers to dial in the load capacity to their requirements in a variety of materials and diameters.
