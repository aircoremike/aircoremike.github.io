The world's finest lightweight bearings balls

Large bearing balls are heavy, but they don’t need to be. Many applications that require large bearing balls don’t require the tremendous load-bearing capacity they offer, so most of the material of a solid steel bearing ball is wasted. You could opt for a lighter material, like titanium or aluminum, but these materials are either too expensive or aren’t hard enough. Or you could choose a hollow ball, but material options and load capacity were limited, until now.
