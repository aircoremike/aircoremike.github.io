Copper

Copper hollow balls provide exceptional electrical and thermal conductivity, excellent formability, and inherent antimicrobial properties. Our unique manufacturing process creates copper bearings that excel in electrical applications and environments requiring antimicrobial surfaces. The superior thermal and electrical conductivity of copper makes these hollow balls ideal for electrical systems, heat transfer applications, and specialized industrial equipment where conductivity is critical. The natural antimicrobial properties add value in medical and food processing applications. With excellent ductility and corrosion resistance, copper hollow balls offer reliable performance in challenging environments while providing the added benefits of natural antimicrobial action and superior heat dissipation capabilities.
