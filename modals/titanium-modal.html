Titanium

Titanium hollow balls offer a remarkable strength-to-weight ratio, excellent corrosion resistance, and superior fatigue life. Our proprietary bonding process creates titanium bearings that outperform traditional alternatives in aerospace and high-performance applications. The exceptional biocompatibility and corrosion resistance of titanium make these hollow balls perfect for medical devices, aerospace systems, and marine applications where material purity and longevity are paramount. Each component is manufactured to aerospace-grade specifications. With outstanding fatigue resistance and temperature stability, titanium hollow balls maintain their performance characteristics across extreme operating conditions, making them the preferred choice for mission-critical applications where failure is not an option.
