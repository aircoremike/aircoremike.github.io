Tungsten

Tungsten hollow balls provide outstanding density and strength, extreme heat resistance, and excellent wear and creep performance. Our specialized manufacturing process creates tungsten bearings that excel in the most demanding high-temperature and high-density applications. The exceptional density and melting point of tungsten make these hollow balls ideal for counterweight applications, radiation shielding, and extreme temperature environments where other materials would fail. Each component meets the highest quality standards for critical applications. With unmatched wear resistance and dimensional stability at extreme temperatures, tungsten hollow balls deliver reliable performance in aerospace, defense, and industrial applications where maximum density and temperature resistance are essential requirements.
