About Us

At AIRCORE, we engineer and manufacture precision lightweight bearing balls designed to reduce system weight without compromising performance. Our products help improve the efficiency, responsiveness, and durability of mechanical assemblies across industries where every gram matters—whether in the sky, on the ground, or in motion.

Our mission is simple: to challenge conventional thinking about bearing components by delivering advanced material solutions that outperform solid counterparts in weight-sensitive environments. From aerospace systems and robotics to medical devices and high-speed automation, AIRCORE hollow balls are helping innovators push limits.

We combine proprietary forming techniques with AI-powered quality control to produce ultra-consistent, high-performance hollow balls in stainless steels, copper alloys, tungsten-based materials, ceramics, and beyond. With a growing catalog of available sizes, wall thicknesses, and material combinations, we’re helping engineers optimize mass without sacrificing mechanical integrity.

At AIRCORE, we don’t just follow specs—we help redefine them. Let’s build lighter, smarter, and stronger together.
